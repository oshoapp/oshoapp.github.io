About OSHOApp

OSHOApp is a simple, modern platform crafted by Extreme_Krish to make Osho's Hindi and English discourses accessible through an advanced, user-friendly reader. Tired of navigating outdated and clunky Osho websites, I built this as a passion project to provide a seamless experience for enthusiasts.

Our advanced reader offers a clean interface, fast navigation, and a distraction-free environment to dive deep into Osho's teachings. The site is non-profit, created purely for fun and to share knowledge. Content is sourced from publicly available discourses under fair use, and we’ll promptly comply with any takedown requests from rights owners.

Explore the source code on GitHub or join our community on Discord to connect with like-minded users.
